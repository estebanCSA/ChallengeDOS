Modern Warfare 2 is one of the best selling games of all time. With it's unique gameplay and giant fan base, you can definitely see why that is. It's replayability is off the charts with multiple game modes. It has a thrilling campaign, intense special ops missions, and diverse multiplayer. It revolutionized the call of duty franchise, with it's new kill streak selection, and amazing visuals at that time. One reason why it is one of my all time favorite games is because of the multiplayer experience with players around the world. I would play this game all the time with my family, and would find it fascinating how a game can be so much fun. Even if I wasn't the greatest it gave me many gaming moments that I have definitely enjoyed. Not to mention that almost the whole game was super balanced, and by that I mean everything was overpowered making it balanced. This is why it is on our list.
